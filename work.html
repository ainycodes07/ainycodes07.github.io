Khiffa Kahva Guide

A 16-page weight loss starter guide written entirely in Urdu — tailored for the brand Khiffa Kahva. Structured as a 3-day plan with daily Khiffa Kahva routines, meal suggestions, light movement, and motivational notes. Culturally sensitive, clear, and actionable.

Content Type

Pages

Language

Deliverable

Scope

The client needed a downloadable PDF guide that introduced Khiffa Kahva as part of a realistic weight loss routine for Pakistani women. They wanted an Urdu-language resource that felt personal, encouraging, and easy to follow — not a clinical manual. The result was a 16-page ebook covering a 3-day starter plan, common mistakes, tips for long-term consistency, and a 3‑day repeat cycle.

Approach
01
Audience & Tone
Studied the target audience — Pakistani women with busy households — and adopted a warm, sisterly tone that motivates without shaming.
02
Structured Outline
Designed a 3-day incremental plan: Day 1 build habits, Day 2 consistency, Day 3 sustained action. Each day covered Kahva intake, simple meal ideas, and gentle movement.
03
Writing & Revisions
Wrote all 16 pages in Urdu. Collaborated closely with the client to ensure accuracy of product usage, health tips, and cultural sensitivity. Refined through multiple revision rounds.
04
Final Polish & Formatting
Delivered a print-ready PDF with clean headings, consistent style, and clear call‑to‑actions embedded in the guide.
